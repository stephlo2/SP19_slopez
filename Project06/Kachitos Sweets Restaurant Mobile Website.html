Kachito’s Sweets is an artisanal pastry shop specializing
in small batch homemade organic sweets that consist
of the finest ingredients.

Kachito’s Sweets grew out of love for baking. Pastry Chef Kathaline Lopez, aka Kachito, always had a passion for baking and selling sweets ever since she can remember. She wasn’t always the pastry expert she is today, Kachito always tells her childhood stories of how she would spend her free time baking cupcakes, however it was always a challenge. However, Kachito would bake and bake until she would get a perfect batch. As the years went by Kachito realized that she was the happiest when she was baking with her mini oven for her family and friends. Kachito’s Sweets created a following little by little and through family and friends and is now one of South Floridas most loved artisinal pastry shops,located in the heart of Wynwood.
